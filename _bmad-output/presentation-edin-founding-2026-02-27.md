## Design Philosophy

**Visual language:** Dark, sophisticated, grounded. Think rich earth tones — deep charcoal backgrounds, warm amber/gold accents, muted greens. No startup neon. No corporate blue. This is a manifesto, not a pitch deck.

**Typography:** One serif typeface for headlines (e.g., Playfair Display, Cormorant Garamond). One clean sans-serif for body text (e.g., Inter, IBM Plex Sans). Large, breathing type. Never more than 30 words on a slide.

**Imagery:** Abstract botanical textures, aerial views of fertile landscapes, macro shots of soil/roots/growth. Avoid stock photos of people. Avoid tech imagery. The visual metaphor is _the garden_, not the machine.

**Transitions:** Simple fades. No animations. Let the words carry the weight.

## Production Notes

### Delivery Guidance

- **Total duration:** 12-15 minutes. Do not rush. This presentation is designed with breathing room.
- **Pacing map:**
  - Slides 1-3 (Hook): 1-2 min — slow, mysterious, magnetic
  - Slides 4-8 (Problem): 3-4 min — building frustration, then the reframe
  - Slides 9-13 (Solution): 3-4 min — intellectual excitement, the "aha"
  - Slides 14-16 (Why You): 2-3 min — intimate, personal, the emotional peak
  - Slides 17-20 (Garden + Close): 2-3 min — poetic, resolute, silence at the end
- **Voice modulation:** Start measured and controlled. Build energy through Act II. Drop to intimate in Act III. Rise to conviction in Act IV. Close with calm certainty.
- **Physical presence:** Stand still for the opening. Move slightly during Act I (frustration = energy). Plant your feet for Act III (directness). Open palms/arms for Act V (invitation).

### Adaptation Notes

- **For video call delivery:** Disable camera on Slides 1-3 to let the visuals speak. Turn camera on at Slide 14 ("Why You") for the personal moment.
- **For in-person delivery:** Dim the lights. The dark backgrounds are designed for low-ambient light. The warm amber accents will glow.
- **For PDF/send-ahead version:** Add the full speaker notes as body text below each slide image. The presentation works as a readable document because the text carries the narrative — the visuals amplify, they don't replace.
- **For smaller groups (3-5 people):** Skip the title card slides (4, 9, 14, 17). Go directly from content to content. More conversational.

### File Formats to Produce

1. **Keynote / PowerPoint / Google Slides** — The primary delivery format
2. **PDF (slide-only)** — For sharing ahead or after
3. **PDF (with notes)** — The send-ahead readable version
4. **Web version** — HTML slides for the Founding Circle portal page (consider reveal.js)
